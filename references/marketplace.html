What is Brightcove Marketplace?

In the bottom-left corner of your app you will see the icon for Brightcove Marketplace under the Admin icon.

Clicking that icon will take you to the Brightcove Marketplace. Brightcove has been partnering with technology providers for years to deliver innovative business outcomes with video. Brightcove Marketplace formalizes and evolves Brightcove's technology partnership program. The Marketplace enables Brightcove to accelerate Brightcove's platform innovation by tapping into technology advancements from our partners and their bespoke solutions.

The Marketplace makes it easy for you to discover and add functionality to the Brightcove platform to solve your specific business challenges. For each listing on the Marketplace, Brightcove emphasizes the benefit of the integration to you and clarifies how it works. Additionally, each integration identifies the APIs it uses and the specific Brightcove products it works with.
